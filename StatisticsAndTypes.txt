Question:- What is Statistics?
Answer: Statistics is the science of collecting , organizing and analyzing the data.

Question:- Types of Statistics?
Answer: There are two types of Statistics.

1.Descriptive Statistics: It consists of organizing and summarizing of data.
a).Measure of Central Tendency.(Mean,Median,Mode)
b).Measure od Dispersion(Variance and Standard Deviation)
c).Different Types of Distribution of data
d).Histogram,pdf,pmf

2.Inferential Statistics: It consists of using data(sample) you have to measure (from population)to form conclusions.
a.)Z-test
b.)T-test
3).ANOVA
etc..
